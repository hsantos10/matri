What to Wear: Embracing Bogotá's Style

Bogotá's weather can be quite variable – think "four seasons in one day" sometimes! As of today, it's about 17°C and partly cloudy, with a similar forecast for tomorrow. Given the elevation and the city's generally cool, often cloudy climate, layering is key. We're aiming for a vibe that's smart casual to cocktail attire, leaning into comfort while still feeling festive.

For Her:

  • Dresses & Skirts: Think knee-length or midi dresses, or stylish skirts paired with a nice top. Fabrics like wool blends, thicker cottons, or even elegant knits work well.
  • Pants: Tailored trousers, smart culottes, or dressy jumpsuits are excellent options.
  • Tops: Pair with blouses, elegant sweaters, or chic long-sleeved tops.
  • Outerwear: This is crucial! Bring a stylish blazer, a trench coat, a nice wool coat, or a chic leather jacket. You'll want it for evenings and potentially cooler daytime moments.
  • Shoes: Comfortable yet elegant block heels, stylish ankle boots, or dressy flats are perfect for navigating the city and dancing. Stilettos might be tricky on uneven sidewalks.
  • Accessories: A nice scarf can add a pop of color and warmth.

For Him:

  • Pants: Dress trousers (wool, linen blends, or smart cottons) or dark, well-fitting jeans for a more casual look.
  • Shirts: Button-down shirts (long-sleeved are best), polos, or fine-gauge knit sweaters.
  • Outerwear: A sport coat, blazer, or a stylish jacket is highly recommended. It adds polish and provides warmth.
  • Shoes: Dress shoes, loafers, or smart, clean sneakers (think fashion sneakers, not gym shoes).

General Tips for Everyone:

  • Layers, Layers, Layers! Bogotá's temperature can drop quickly, especially in the evenings. Having a cardigan, sweater, or jacket on hand is essential.
  • Comfort is Key: While we want you to look great, we also want you to be comfortable as we celebrate, eat, and potentially explore.
  • Embrace Color & Texture: Colombians love to dress well, and often incorporate rich colors and interesting textures into their outfits. Don't shy away from adding some flair!
